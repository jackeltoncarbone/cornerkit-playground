CornerKit

Arc-free superellipse corners (the math Apple's squircle is built on) emitted as pure béziers into an SVG clip-path — Round, Pill, Circle, Scoop and Notch on one smoothing curve. A pill is just the same curve at radius = height/2, so it elongates with smoothing instead of snapping to a plain stadium. Every tile is real glass (backdrop-filter) with a cast shadow and an SVG rim.

Modes — one hero per shape

Mass — density with live glass, shadow & auto shape detection
